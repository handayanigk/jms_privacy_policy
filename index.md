JMSPDAM – Internal Employee Application

Effective Date: 25 June 2026

Privacy Policy

JMSPDAM is an internal mobile application developed and operated by PDAM Tirta Handayani. This application is intended exclusively for authorized employees and is not available for public use.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected while using the application.

Information We Collect

To perform employee duties, the application may collect:

Employee account information
Employee ID
Name
Work unit
Login credentials
Device information
Application usage logs

Depending on the features enabled, the application may also access:

Camera
Storage
Internet connection
Location (only if required for work purposes)
Purpose of Data Collection

Information is collected solely to:

Authenticate employees
Provide access to internal PDAM systems
Display operational information
Record employee activities related to assigned duties
Improve application performance and security
Troubleshoot technical issues
Location Data

If location permission is granted, location information is used only for operational features required by PDAM and is not continuously tracked outside the application's functions.

Camera and Storage

Camera and storage permissions are used only for work-related purposes such as:

Taking documentation photos
Uploading supporting files
Scanning QR Codes
Downloading work documents

The application does not access unrelated personal files.

Data Sharing

Employee information is not sold or shared with third parties.

Information is accessible only by authorized PDAM personnel and systems necessary to operate the application, unless disclosure is required by applicable law.

Data Security

PDAM implements reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect employee information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary to support operational requirements and comply with applicable regulations.

Third-Party Services

Depending on the application configuration, JMSPDAM may use services such as:

Google Play Services
Firebase Cloud Messaging
Google Maps

These services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Employee Responsibilities

Employees are responsible for:

Maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.
Reporting unauthorized access immediately.
Using the application only for official PDAM business.
Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect operational or legal requirements.
